Article: How Covid-19 Has Turned the Spotlight Back on Obesity

This article talks about the link between obesity and Covid-19. The article mentions that 39% of adults in the United States (1.9 billion people) were overweight in 2016. The article mentions that there are a large variety of factors that can cause obesity such as diet, genetics, lack of exercise, and social environment. The article also mention the economic factors that also influence ongoing obesity rates.
